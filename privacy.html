LintMail – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

LintMail does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All HTML analysis is performed locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device. The extension has no backend server, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services of any kind.

The only data stored is your language preference (English or Portuguese), saved locally via chrome.storage.local and never transmitted anywhere.
